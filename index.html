The manifesto

A lot of creators use AI. For drafts, mockups, references, captions, edits, code, outlines, thumbnails, workflows, and getting past the blank page.

Then they hide it, because the internet turned process into a purity test. Stop AI Hate is for the people who still make the thing.

AI-assisted does not mean effortless. The tool did not make the taste. The prompt did not replace judgment. The machine did not care enough to finish it.
